Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you download

Is Stock Score free to download?

Yes — Stock Score is free to download on iOS and Android. Core features are available at no cost. A premium subscription unlocks the full suite of advanced analytics, screeners, and deeper insights.

What is a stock score?

A stock score is an objective 0–10 rating generated by our proprietary algorithm, which analyses hundreds of data points — including valuation metrics, profitability ratios, growth indicators, insider sentiment, and analyst ratings — to give you a clear picture of any stock's potential.

Which stocks does Stock Score cover?

Stock Score covers thousands of global stocks across major markets including the US, UK, and beyond. Search and score individual stocks, discover opportunities via screeners, and track earnings for companies worldwide.

Is Stock Score available on Android?

Yes — Stock Score is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Download it free on either platform.

Does Stock Score provide financial advice?

No. Stock Score provides data, analytics, and scoring tools to help you research stocks more efficiently. It does not constitute financial advice. All investment decisions are your own — investment involves risk and past performance does not guarantee future results.
